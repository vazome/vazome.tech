In infrastructure Stack model the usage and responsibility is defined per unit of consumption

Unit of consumption is an entity you consume and take responsibility for, e.g.
-   If you watch Netflix, then the service itself is the unit of consumption.
	-   You have subscribed to Netflix, and it's your responsibility to manage your account.
-   If you deploy a VM, then operation system is the unit of consumption.
	-   You created VM, and it's you responsibility to manage the OS

Service models we use today are coming from the evolutions of the classic infrastructure stack.
